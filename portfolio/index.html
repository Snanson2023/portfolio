About Me

Hi, my name is Sam and I am a Building Maintenance Technician from South Wales. Although my background is in maintenance, I have always had a strong passion for coding and technology. After years of putting it off, I decided to take the leap and pursue my passion for coding by enrolling in a diploma program for web and software development.


Currently, I am working my way through the Full-Stack course on Codecademy with a goal of becoming a well-rounded developer. My ultimate goal is to start a Mechatronics Engineering apprenticeship and further my knowledge in the field of engineering while improving my coding skills on the side.


When I'm not tinkering with code or maintaining buildings, you can usually find me gaming or spending some quality time with my daughter. I'm excited for the opportunities and challenges that the world of programming and engineering has to offer, and I can't wait to see where this journey takes me.
